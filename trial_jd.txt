# Senior Data Scientist - AI/ML Team

**Company:** TechFlow Analytics  
**Location:** San Francisco, CA (Remote Friendly)  
**Experience Level:** 5+ years  

## About the Role

We are seeking a highly skilled Senior Data Scientist to join our AI/ML team and drive data-driven decision making across our organization. You will work on cutting-edge machine learning projects, from recommendation systems to predictive analytics, and help scale our data science capabilities.

## Key Responsibilities

- Design and implement machine learning models for business applications
- Analyze large datasets to extract actionable insights and patterns
- Build and maintain scalable data pipelines and ML infrastructure
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams including engineering, product, and business teams
- Mentor junior data scientists and establish best practices
- Present findings and recommendations to senior leadership
- Stay current with latest developments in AI/ML and implement innovative solutions

## Required Qualifications

- Master's or PhD in Data Science, Computer Science, Statistics, Mathematics, or related field
- 5+ years of experience in data science and machine learning
- Strong proficiency in Python and SQL
- Experience with machine learning frameworks (scikit-learn, TensorFlow, PyTorch)
- Expertise in statistical analysis and experimental design
- Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, or Azure)
- Proficiency with data manipulation tools (pandas, numpy)
- Strong understanding of A/B testing and statistical inference
- Experience with big data technologies (Spark, Hadoop) is a plus

## Preferred Qualifications

- Experience with deep learning and neural networks
- Knowledge of MLOps practices and model deployment
- Experience with recommendation systems or NLP
- Publications in peer-reviewed conferences or journals
- Experience with containerization (Docker, Kubernetes)
- Background in time series analysis or forecasting
- Experience leading cross-functional data science projects

## Technical Skills

- **Programming:** Python, SQL, R (nice to have)
- **ML** TensorFlow, PyTorch, XGBoost
- **Big Data:** Apache Spark, Hadoop, Databricks
- **Cloud:** AWS (S3, EC2, SageMaker), GCP, Azure
- **Databases:** PostgreSQL, MySQL, MongoDB, Redshift
- **Tools:** Jupyter, Git, Docker, Airflow, MLflow
